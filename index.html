Frequently Asked Questions

How to cancel?

We understand plans can change! To cancel your booking or reservation, simply visit our Cancellation Section, enter your Booking ID/Reservation ID and the email address you used for the booking. If you need further assistance, feel free to reach out to us—we’re always happy to help.

Is there any vacancy?

We’d love to have you stay with us or dine at our restaurant! To check current room availability or table reservations, please visit our Booking/Reservation Section or contact us directly. We’ll ensure you find the perfect spot for your plans.

What are payment methods?

We strive to make payments easy and secure for you. We accept: Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, etc.) UPI Payments Online Wallets (PayPal, Google Pay, etc.) Bank Transfers (for special cases) For any payment-related queries, feel free to contact us.

How to claim coupon codes?

Enjoying savings is simple! During the booking or reservation process, you’ll see a field labeled “Apply Coupon”. Enter your code, and the discount will automatically apply. If you encounter any issues, let us know, and we’ll ensure you get the best deal.

What are the age requirements?

We want everyone to feel welcome! However, guests under 18 years must be accompanied by a parent or guardian. For dining reservations, all ages are welcome. If you have specific concerns, feel free to reach out—we’d be delighted to assist.
